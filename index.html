Adjacent Reality

Adjacent Reality is a riff on Stuart Kauffman's adjacent possible. We formed it to explore and make possible the realities slightly adjacent to our own, primarily through hacks and inventions that bridge the virtual and the real.

In more grounded terms, we are building open source human computer interaction hardware and writing nifty software to go with it. Our first project is Tracker, a tiny, inexpensive, wirelessly interfaced inertial measurement unit, or motion/orientation tracker. We'll have more friendly documentation as the project gets closer to complete, but in the mean time, you can follow our progress on GitHub.
